# Amazon Product Link Workflow

Use your Amazon.com Associates account with Associate ID `tidysmallspac-20`.

## Create the first three links

1. Open `content/amazon-product-links.csv`.
2. Search Amazon.com using the phrase in the `Amazon Search Phrase` column.
3. Open one suitable product detail page.
4. Check the product measurements and the notes in `Checks Before Adding`.
5. In the Amazon SiteStripe bar, choose **Get Link > Text**.
6. Copy the generated SiteStripe link.
7. Send the product name and SiteStripe text link to Codex.

## Guardrails

- Choose products that solve the specific problem in the guide.
- Do not copy Amazon product images, star ratings, review text, or prices manually.
- Do not buy through your own affiliate links.
- Do not ask friends or relatives to buy through the links.
- Keep Pinterest traffic organic. Do not boost Pins that lead to Amazon-linked pages.
