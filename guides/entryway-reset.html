Quick reset

A 20-minute apartment entrance reset

A tiny entrance can become useful again in one short session. Focus on the things that cross the threshold every day.

I may earn a commission from qualifying purchases through links in this guide.

Set a timer

  1. Minutes 1-4: remove anything that belongs in another room.
  2. Minutes 5-8: keep only the shoes currently in rotation.
  3. Minutes 9-12: choose one visible tray for keys and small essentials.
  4. Minutes 13-16: give everyday bags one easy hook or shelf.
  5. Minutes 17-20: add one basket for mail and decide when to empty it.
Optional tray to consider:

A small catch-all tray can make the key-and-small-items step easier to maintain. Check the tray dimensions against your available surface.

Keep the reset small

Do not redesign the entire entrance. Solve the daily pile first, then add storage only if a specific item still has no home.
