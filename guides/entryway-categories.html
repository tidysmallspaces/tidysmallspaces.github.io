Landing-zone guide

Five things every small entryway needs a home for

A tidy entrance does not need much furniture. It needs clear homes for the items that arrive with you and leave with you.

Organize the recurring categories

  1. Keys and small items: use one tray in the easiest place to reach.
  2. Shoes: keep only the current rotation by the door.
  3. Bags: use one hook or shelf position for each everyday bag.
  4. Mail: capture it in one basket and process it on a fixed day.
  5. Weather gear: group umbrellas, hats, and reusable bags by season.
