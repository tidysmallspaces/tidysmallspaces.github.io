Room-by-room guide

Seven empty spaces renters forget to use

A small home may have more usable room than it appears to have. Walk through these areas before adding furniture or another set of storage bins.

I may earn a commission from qualifying purchases through links in this guide.

Check these areas

  1. The back of a closet or bedroom door.
  2. The space under a bed, sofa, or raised cabinet.
  3. The area above an existing shelf.
  4. The inside of a cabinet door.
  5. A narrow gap beside an appliance or wardrobe.
  6. The floor beneath hanging clothes in a closet.
  7. The top of a wardrobe for light, occasional-use items.

Use less than you find

You do not need to fill every gap. Choose the areas that solve a real daily problem and leave breathing room elsewhere.
