Safer storage

A safer vertical-storage plan for a small home

Vertical storage can free up valuable floor space, but the highest shelf is not the right place for everything. Plan by weight, frequency, and ease of access.

Place items from low to high

  • Keep heavy, awkward, or fragile items on lower shelves.
  • Store daily-use items between waist and shoulder height when possible.
  • Use upper shelves for light, occasional-use categories.
  • Respect the stated weight limit for every removable hook, rack, and shelf.
  • Avoid loose overhead storage above a bed, sofa, or frequently used seat.
Optional organizer to consider:

An over-door organizer is one removable way to use vertical space for lightweight items. Confirm its dimensions, installation method, and intended load before using it.

Keep retrieval simple

Use labels and stable containers. If you cannot safely reach an item, move it lower or choose a different storage location.
