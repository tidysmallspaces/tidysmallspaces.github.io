Measure-first checklist

Under-bed and vertical-storage checklist

The best small-space storage starts with a tape measure. Use this checklist before adding containers, shelves, or hooks.

I may earn a commission from qualifying purchases through links in this guide.

Under the bed

  • Measure the usable height at the lowest point, including any center support.
  • Measure width and depth, leaving enough room to pull a container out.
  • Store only clean, dry items that make sense to access occasionally.
  • Label the outward-facing edge so you do not need to open every container.

Vertical storage

  • Separate daily-access items from occasional-access items.
  • Check the product weight limit and the surface requirements.
  • Keep heavy items lower and avoid placing loose items above a bed or seat.
  • Use consistent categories so items are easy to return to their place.
